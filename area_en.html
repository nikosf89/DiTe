Why Kifissia is the ideal place for real estate investments.

Discover the unique features that make Kifissia an ideal place for real estate

1 Location

Kifissia is one of the most prestigious areas of Athens, which has luxurious villas, luxury shops and picturesque parks. Being just 12 kilometres from the city centre, it offers both tranquility and easy access to urban life.

Source: athensattica.com

2 High demand.

The attractiveness of Kifissia among locals and expatriates ensures a consistently high demand for real estate, promising high rental yields and capital appreciation.

Source: kifissia.gr

3 High quality luxuries

With its luxury shops, gourmet restaurants and upscale boutiques, Kifissia promises a high standard of living. Its top schools further enhance its appeal to families.

Source: athinorama.gr

4 Green spaces

Kifissia's green parks offer residents a peaceful refuge from the urban bustle, increasing its attractiveness as a residential and investment option.

Source: kidshub.gr

5 Means of Transport

Well connected to the metro, Kifissia ensures easy access to the city centre and other areas of Athens.

Source: ypaithros.gr

6 History

Rich in history, Kifissia has attractions such as the Municipal Park and the historic railway station, adding charm and attracting tourists.

Source: foursquare.com

7 Development Opportunities

While Kifissia is already a sought-after location, it promises further further growth. From the renovation of older properties to anticipated new projects, the area offers both short-term and long-term investment benefits.

In essence, with a mix of luxury, demand and possibilities, Kifissia is undoubtedly a top choice for real estate investment.
