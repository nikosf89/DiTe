Kifissia

The Jewel of the Northern Suburbs of Athens

Kifissia is a prominent residential area in the northern suburbs of Athens, Greece:

Suburban Atmosphere

It is known for its affluent atmosphere, lush greenery and elegant mansions. Within this area, the apartment you see gives you an advantageous position.

Excellent Infrastructure

The area has an excellent infrastructure, with easy access to the city centre and to other parts of Athens. The Kifissia Avenue allows for convenient transportation and the railway station serves as a suburban train station.

Source: el.wikiloc.com

Peaceful Environment

Kifissia offers a peaceful environment with beautiful parks and gardens, including the popular Kifisia Grove.

Source: wikimapia.org

Live Market Scene

There is a lively shopping scene on the main shopping street, Kifissia Avenue, with boutiques, fashion shops, cafes, restaurants and bars.

Source: primerealestate.gr

Famous Schools

The area is home to prestigious schools, making it attractive for families seeking quality education.

Source: Vresto.gr

Various Amenities

Residents have access to various amenities, including medical facilities, gyms, sports clubs, a bicycle path and recreational activities.

Source: kifisialife.gr

Cultural Events

The neighbourhood also hosts cultural events and festivals.

Source: paron.gr

Security

Safety is a remarkable advantage of living in Kifissia because of the low crime rates and well-maintained streets.
